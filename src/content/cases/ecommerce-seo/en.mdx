# E-commerce SEO Optimization

## Challenge

A large electronics online store faced low organic traffic and poor search engine rankings.

## Solution

We conducted comprehensive SEO optimization:

### Technical Optimization

- Fixed indexing issues
- Optimized URL structure
- Improved loading speed
- Configured proper redirects

### Content Strategy

- Created unique descriptions for all categories
- Optimized meta tags
- Added structured data
- Implemented internal linking

### Conversion Optimization

- Improved UX on product pages
- Optimized checkout process
- Added reviews and ratings

## Results

- **+300%** organic traffic in 6 months
- **+150%** conversions from organic search
- **Top-3** positions for key queries
- **+200%** revenue from SEO traffic
