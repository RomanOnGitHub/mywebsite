# Content Strategy for SaaS Startup

## Challenge

A SaaS startup in project management needed a strategy to attract customers through content.

## Solution

Developed a comprehensive content strategy:

### Audience Analysis

- Created detailed buyer personas
- Studied target audience pain points
- Identified key communication channels

### Content Plan

- Weekly blog articles
- Monthly webinars
- Regular email newsletters
- Active social media presence

### SEO Optimization

- Keyword research
- Search query optimization
- Landing page creation
- Internal linking

## Results

- **+500%** organic traffic in a year
- **+250%** leads from content
- **+180%** trial conversions
- **Top-10** for 50+ key queries
